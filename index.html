Crystals Collector

Step 1: You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
Step 2: There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
Step 3: You win the game by mathching your total score to the random number. You lose that game if your total score goes above the random number.
Step 4: The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Final Step! Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
